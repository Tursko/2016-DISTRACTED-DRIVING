Eating While Driving

Believe it or not, eating while driving is a serious problem that is not thought of very often when people get behind the wheel. Most people think that it really is not that big of an issue because they know where their mouth is and they do not have to look down at their food, therefore they aren’t distracted. But often times, people do have to look down at their food to make sure the amount of food will fit in their mouth or to check if anything dropped out of or off of the piece of food they were holding. Not only that but once the food is in your mouth you risk the chance of choking, thus causing another problem that could affect your driving. The food can wait, we have ways of reheating for a reason.
