Texting and Driving

Texting and driving is one problem that has not been around very long but has been a massive problem in our society recently. Studies show that in 2011, nearly a quarter of collisions on the road were involving cell phones. Since then, technology has gotten better, making people think that it is now safer to text and drive. Because of this, a 2012 study shows that over 2/3 of drivers between the ages of 18 and 20 had admitted to using their cell phones while driving. This is a problem that needs to be taken care of, so please don’t text and drive. It can wait.
